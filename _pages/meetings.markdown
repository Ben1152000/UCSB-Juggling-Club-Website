🎉🎉🎉 *We have returned to in-person meetings for the 2021-2022 school year!* 🎉🎉🎉

- Fridays from 3pm-5pm on the lawn at the base of Storke Tower.
- Saturdays from 3pm until sunset at Willow Glen Park in Santa Barbara.
